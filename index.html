Experience our patient-centered care

We have a 50 bed health facility that was established in 1968. Over the years, St. Nicholas Hospital has expanded to become a first class health care provider offering a wide range of services and the preferred referral medical facility for both local and international organizations.

Our reputation as one of the leading healthcare providers was confirmed with our kidney transplant unit which has successfully conducted over 80% of all Kidney transplants in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Our highly skilled medical staff strength comprises full time consultants, several visiting consultants in different specialties, Registrars, Medical Officers, House Officers and over 100 double qualified nurses. Our healthcare professionals maintain a strong focus on quality care and constantly ensure their commitment to patient’s safety.

Dr Ebun Bamgboye Clinical Director
